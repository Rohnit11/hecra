8. Coverage Assessment & Saturation Statement
Keyword matrix: All 8 keyword terms from the Klaus Miller Protocol §2.2 have been executed across SSRN, arXiv, Google Scholar, and domain-specific repositories in both Round 1 and Round 2.
Snowballing: Backward snowballing from all 3 seed papers is complete. Forward snowballing has been partially executed (constrained by web search tool vs. full Google Scholar API). No new T1 or T2 papers were identified in Round 2 that had not already appeared via Round 1 snowballing, with the exception of Khosravi & Yoganarasimhan (2026) — which was a Round 2 primary find and is now flagged as the highest-priority paper in the corpus.
Saturation: Round 2 returned 10 new accepted papers. Of these, 4 were T1, 3 were T2, and 3 were T3. The marginal yield of T1 papers declined relative to Round 1, consistent with approaching saturation in the core empirical literature. Recommended: one further targeted round focusing on (a) forward snowball of Khosravi & Yoganarasimhan (2026) and (b) AEA 2026 conference program papers not yet on SSRN.
